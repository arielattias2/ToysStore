Sign Up:/users/signUp (POST)

  • role

    • Sign Up:/users/signIn (POST)
  • email (required)
  • password (required)

    • Add product - (only for register user - must send user's auth-token through headers):/toys/myinfo (GET)

  • Get 10 products by page number:/toys?p=number (GET)
  • Get X products in each page:/toys?pp=number (GET)
  • Search products by name or info:/toys?s=key-word (GET)
  • Sort products:/toys?sort=sort-key (GET)
  • Reverse by sort key:/toys?r=y (GET)
  • Price range:/toys?min=number&max=number (GET)
  • Get all products by category:/toys/category/:category-name (GET)
